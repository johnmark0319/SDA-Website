SDA FAMILY
SDA (Sto. Domingo Associates), founded by Josefino “Pepot” Sto. Domingo in 2005, stands as one of Philam Life’s largest and most successful agencies. Our ongoing mission is to recruit and nurture a select group of financial advisors and agency leaders, dedicated to serving the Filipino market through Philam Life’s diverse range of products and services. SDA’s overarching objective is to cultivate a new generation of skilled financial advisors who can offer tailored solutions for the financial protection and security of Filipino families. Over the past 11 years, SDA has expanded its footprint beyond its initial location in Makati, now maintaining a robust presence in Metro Manila, Pampanga, Cebu, Bohol, and Dumaguete.
PHILIPPINE AMERICAN LIFE AND GENERAL INSURANCE COMPANY
AIA Philippines, formerly known as AIA Philam Life, boasts a storied 74-year legacy. As a premier life insurance company, it is committed to empowering Filipinos by offering comprehensive protection and long-term savings solutions. Philam Life’s holistic portfolio includes life protection, health insurance, education plans, retirement options, group coverage, and credit life insurance. Additionally, it collaborates with its subsidiaries, BPI Philam Life Assurance Company (BPLAC) and Philam Asset Management Inc. (PAMI), to provide bancassurance and fund management services.
Philam Life proudly stands as a member of AIA Group Limited—a beacon in the vast expanse of pan-Asian life insurance. With unwavering commitment, it navigates the currents of financial security, serving as a guardian for millions across the region. AIA Group’s legacy echoes through Philam Life’s corridors, where promises are etched, and futures find their anchor.
AIA PHILIPPINES LIFE AND GENERAL INSURANCE COMPANY
AIA Philam Life, now known as AIA Philippines, has a rich history spanning 74 years. As a premier life insurance company, it aims to empower Filipinos by providing protection and long-term savings solutions. With PHP 291.63 billion in total assets and PHP 80.20 billion in net worth (as of December 2020), AIA Philippines continues to build trust through its financial strength and commitment to delivering on promises. Collaborating with Bank of the Philippine Islands (BPI), it offers insurance products with health coverage and investment options. AIA Philippines emphasizes efficiency, providing protection in just 20 minutes. Remember, life insurance secures your family’s financial future, so choose wisely based on your needs and goals. If you have specific questions, consider reaching out to AIA Philippines directly.
